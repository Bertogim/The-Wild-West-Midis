Why i made this page:

In The Wild West i love midis, i made a midi list trough discord, then discord didn't work so i made another list on google pages using google drive but it was hard to do, a single midi could take 2 minutes uploading it, extracting the midi data and putting it to the midi list so i made this page where its automatic, every midi i upload adds to the list and its way much easier to ccopy midis from here, add favorites...
